## Risks & Mitigation

### Risk: Learning too much too fast
**Mitigation:** Focus on practical application, not just theory. Pair with Tech Lead frequently.

### Risk: Code review quality suffers
**Mitigation:** Tech Lead reviews your reviews initially. Gradual increase in responsibility.

### Risk: Not enough time for learning
**Mitigation:** Block 2 hours/day for learning. Protect this time.

### Risk: Imposter syndrome
**Mitigation:** Remember you're learning. Ask questions. Pair programming helps.
