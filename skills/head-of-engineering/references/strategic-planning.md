# Strategic Planning Framework

## Long-Term Vision Setting

### 6-Month Planning Horizon
- Define technical roadmap
- Identify major initiatives
- Allocate resources strategically
- Set measurable goals

### 12-Month Strategic Goals
- Technology modernization
- Team capability development
- Infrastructure improvements
- Process optimization

## OKR Framework

**Objectives:** Qualitative goals
**Key Results:** Quantitative metrics

**Example:**
```
Objective: Improve system reliability
Key Results:
- 99.9% uptime
- <5 minute MTTR
- Zero data loss incidents
```

## Resource Allocation

### Budget Management
- Infrastructure costs
- Tool and service subscriptions
- Training and development
- Contingency reserves (10-15%)

### Team Capacity Planning
- Current utilization rates
- Skill gaps identification
- Hiring needs assessment
- Training priorities

## Risk Management

### Risk Categories
1. **Technical Risks:** Technology obsolescence, technical debt
2. **Resource Risks:** Key person dependencies, capacity constraints
3. **External Risks:** Vendor dependencies, market changes
4. **Security Risks:** Vulnerabilities, compliance issues

### Risk Mitigation Strategies
- Identify and assess risks quarterly
- Prioritize by impact × probability
- Develop mitigation plans
- Monitor and review regularly

## Stakeholder Management

### Communication Cadence
- Weekly: Team updates
- Monthly: Progress reports
- Quarterly: Strategic reviews
- Ad-hoc: Critical issues

### Stakeholder Matrix
| Stakeholder | Interest | Influence | Strategy |
|-------------|----------|-----------|----------|
| CEO | High | High | Manage closely |
| CFO | Medium | High | Keep satisfied |
| Users | High | Low | Keep informed |

## Performance Metrics

### Team Performance
- Velocity and throughput
- Quality metrics
- Cycle time
- Team satisfaction

### Business Impact
- Feature adoption rates
- User satisfaction scores
- Revenue impact
- Cost savings

## Decision-Making Framework

### Strategic Decisions
1. Gather data and context
2. Consult relevant stakeholders
3. Analyze options and trade-offs
4. Make decision with clear rationale
5. Communicate decision
6. Monitor outcomes
7. Adjust if needed

### Decision Documentation
- Context and background
- Options considered
- Decision made
- Rationale
- Expected outcomes
- Review date
